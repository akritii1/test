College of Technology is affiliated to Andhra University and was established in 1980. college institutions include college Institute of Technology, college Institute of Science, college Institute of Management, college School of International Business, college Institute of Pharmacy, college School of Architecture, college School of Law, college Institute of Medical Sciences and Research, college School of Nursing, Centre for Distance Learning (CDL) and college School of Gandhian Studies. The university offers 109 programs at undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral levels in Technology, Pharmacy, Science, Management, International Business, Architecture and Law. Admission to college is through the university entrance exam GAT (college Admission Test), a computer based online test which is held across major cities in India. Based on ranks achieved, admissions are given in Visakhapatnam campus and Hyderabad campus. Admissions to other institutes are done through their entrance exams like GSAT for college Institute of Science, GLAT for the School of Law. Jee Paper-2, NATA for Architecture, etc.
